Who We Are

Mobile Physio is a credited physiotherapist clinic specialising in Deep Tissue Massage, Injury Rehabilitation and Sports Massage.

About Us:

Deep Tissue Massage:

We are experts in this massage technique, which applies pressure to multiple layers of muscle and the tissue connecting it.

This treatment is useful for treating strains and sports related injuries. Deep tissue massage will also improve injury recovery times by increasing blood flow to the affected area.

Injury Rehabilitation:

We specialise creating rehabilitation plans tailor made to your specific injury and fitness level, allowing you the best chances at an effective recovery

We also offer pain management treatments to help decrease pain and relive swelling.

Sports Massage:

Our sports massage techniques can be tailored to specific injuries or sporting activities, improving heart rate and increasing circulation to the affected area.

Targeting specific trigger points, we can assist in relieving pain and tension.
